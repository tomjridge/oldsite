PhD students

News update: loans will be made available for PhD study in the UK!

The government will offer very generous “loans” of £25,000 to people wanting to study for a PhD in the UK.

https://www.gov.uk/government/uploads/system/uploads/attachment_data/file/597333/Doctoral_response_to_consultation.pdf

The loans should become available in Spring/Summer 2018 for applications for 2018/19 academic year.

Introduction

I am always looking to recruit PhD students. I am happy to consider any topic, but for best results, the topic should be in an area close to my current interests (look at my recent publications, and my home page).

The department has EPSRC studentships (for UK students) and GTA positions (for EU students, requires a small amount of work helping out in labs). These provide full tuition and living expenses for the duration of the PhD.

The following are links to University pages with some more information:

  • https://www2.le.ac.uk/departments/computer-science/postgraduate/research/interests - interests of members of the department; search for “Ridge”

  • https://www2.le.ac.uk/departments/computer-science/postgraduate/research/topics - research topics of members of the department; probably out of date

  • https://www2.le.ac.uk/departments/computer-science/research/rthemes - research themes of the department

  • http://www2.le.ac.uk/departments/computer-science/postgraduate/funding - links to current positions (updated 2016-06-22 with a PhD position available from Oct; apply by July 22)

  • http://www2.le.ac.uk/offices/jobs/opportunities/jobsearch - new GTA positions are advertised here

In the first instance, please email tr61 (at) le.ac.uk a CV to express interest. Ideally also include a brief description of what you would like to study.
