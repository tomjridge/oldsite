Giving world online

During 2011/2012 I helped Giving world online implement a customer relationship management system based on SugarCRM. This involved porting their existing data (mostly in spreadsheets) to Sugar (backed by MySQL). Then various Sugar modules were implemented to customize Sugar to their situation. I was reasonably impressed by SugarCRM.
